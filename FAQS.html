What are NFTs / What is Minting?

A non-fungible token (NFT) is a cryptographic token that represents a unique asset. They function as verifiable proofs of authenticity and ownership within a blockchain network. NFTs are not interchangeable with each other and introduce scarcity to the digital world. Minting is defined as the computer process of validating information, creating a new block and recording that information into the blockchain.


What are Smart Contracts?

Smart contracts are simply programs stored on a blockchain that run when predetermined conditions are met.


What is a Metaverse?

A metaverse, short for "meta-universe," is a digital world where the real and virtual merge into a vision of science fiction and allows people to move between different devices and communicate in a virtual environment.
